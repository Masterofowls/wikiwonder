{{ SITE_NAME }} is an open wiki knowledge base where you can publish encyclopedia-style articles, organize topics into categories, and collect bookmarks for offline reading. Every wiki page supports rich markdown, multilingual translations, and structured sections so long-form reference material stays easy to navigate. Whether you are documenting a hobby, curating research notes, or building a team handbook, the platform keeps your content searchable and shareable.

Use the explore tab to browse the latest wiki articles, or jump to featured and trending pages when you want a quick overview of popular topics.
